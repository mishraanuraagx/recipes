Ingredients

    1 cup Milk
    1 ½ tablespoons Sugar powdered
    1 ½ to 2 teaspoons Instant coffee powder
    4-5 Ice cubes

Instructions

  1.  Take everything (milk, coffee, sugar, ice) into the blender.

  2.  Blend it for a minute until it gets frothy.

  3.  Pour it into a big serving glass or mug.

  4.  Garnish with cocoa powder or chocolate sprinkles. This is optional.

  5.  Serve immediately.
